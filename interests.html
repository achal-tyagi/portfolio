Interests

The routines, moments, and pursuits that keep me energised, grounded, and curious outside work.

Competitive Football

Sport has been a defining pillar of my discipline. Having represented my school and academy at the National level in 10th grade, as well as competing in CBSE Clusters and Zonal tournaments, football taught me the core mechanics of high-pressure teamwork and strategic execution. These skills translate directly into how I approach complex business problems today.

Skies & Stillness

Amidst a fast-paced academic and professional schedule, I find immense inspiration in the quiet aesthetics of nature. Taking a moment to observe a vibrant sunrise or the shifting canvas of an intricate sky provides a necessary mental reset, keeping me grounded, observant, and deeply appreciative of the calm before a busy week.

The Fitness Journey

My fitness journey began from a deeply personal place—I used to be very skinny and wanted to fundamentally change my physique. Over time, what started as a desire to build strength evolved into a cornerstone of my daily routine. Today, whether through rigorous strength training or long runs, physical wellbeing is my foundation for mental clarity and sustained resilience.

Canine Companionship

On a more personal note, I have a profound love for dogs. Their genuine warmth, loyalty, and uncomplicated presence bring a necessary and joyful balance to my life. Stepping away from screens to play or simply spend quiet time with them is the ultimate stress-reliever and the perfect counterweight to a structured and analytical professional world.
